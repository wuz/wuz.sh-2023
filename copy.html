Copy What You Want

I believe in freely sharing work, especially when it has the potential to help folks that are still learning. All the code and content on this site is freely shared. Remix, reuse, copy, steal - whatever you want. I do ask that if you use my content, you include attributions, but you can feel free to make changes if you want. Just be sure to specify what parts you added and what parts are mine!
